## Beginner / Onboarding Guide

### What is a memecoin?
- A token driven mostly by attention/community
- Why it can move fast (and why it can crash fast)

### What is a “call”?
- A public “I’m tracking/buying/watching this token” moment
- Why entry timing matters

### What is market cap?
- A rough size metric: price × supply
- Why it’s used instead of “price” alone

### What does 2x / 10x mean?
- “2x” = doubled from a reference point
- “10x” = ten times the reference point
- Why big multipliers are rare and high-risk

### Why people use X (Twitter) for crypto
- Speed and visibility
- Pros/cons vs Discord

### Risks of the space
- Scams, rugs, fake volume, spoofed socials
- Liquidity risk and slippage
- Overconfidence and narrative traps

### How McGBot helps
- Makes calls trackable and comparable
- Adds moderation + trust layers
- Helps communities focus on outcomes over hype
