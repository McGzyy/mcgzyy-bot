## Explanation / Showcase Guide

### What McGBot is
- A tracking + moderation system for coin calls
- Designed to separate signal from noise over time

### What makes it different
- Identity linkage (Discord + verified X)
- Stats-informed recognition (Top Caller) without full automation
- Centralized moderation workflow (`#mod-approvals`)

### Why tracking matters
- Calls are cheap; outcomes are measurable
- Consistent rules improve trust and comparability

### Trust layers and signal quality
- `top_caller` (earned / stats-informed)
- `trusted_pro` (curated / higher-trust submission capability)
- Why these are intentionally different

### High-level system explanation
- Intake (Discord commands, X mentions)
- Tracking + monitoring loop
- Moderation gates (approval flows)
- Reporting surfaces (stats, leaderboards, review cards)

### Why this is useful / powerful
- Better moderation ergonomics
- Higher-quality public feed
- Clearer incentives for good behavior
